Higher Wins
Higher Wins is a card game where you play against the computer.

The higher card is the one with the number 12 and the lower one is the one with the number 1. There are 2 cards of the same number. Both players get 8 cards and the rest of the deck is out of the game. Both players draw a card simultaneously. The one that has the higher card wins.
